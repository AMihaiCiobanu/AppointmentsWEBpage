Appointments & Reports

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026. This policy covers both the Appointments & Reports app (Android and iOS) and this website.

Who we are

Appointments & Reports is an independent app developed by an individual developer. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: c_mihail@icloud.com.

Data collected by the app

The app stores the following data that you enter yourself:

  • Clients: name, phone number, email address, birthday, and notes.
  • Appointments: date, time, duration, service, price, and notes.
  • Financial records: income entries and expenses you add manually.
  • Settings: your preferences, working hours, reminder configurations, and service catalogue.

All of this data is created by you and belongs to you. We do not collect it, read it, or process it — it stays on your device.

Where your data is stored

  • Locally on your device — all data is stored on your phone or tablet by default. The app works fully offline.
  • Firebase (Google Cloud) — optional — if you choose to sign in with your Google account, your data is synced to your personal Firebase account under your own user ID. This is used only to keep your data in sync across your own devices. You can disable this at any time from the app settings.

Contacts permission (Android only)

  • The Android version can access your device contacts only when you explicitly tap "Import from Contacts" to create a new client.
  • Only the selected contact's name, phone number, and email are used. Your full contacts list is never uploaded or stored.

Biometric data (iOS only)

If you enable Face ID or Touch ID lock, biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system (Apple's LocalAuthentication framework). No biometric data is ever accessed, stored, or transmitted by the app.

Third-party services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In / Firebase — for optional authentication and cloud sync. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

No advertising SDKs or data brokers are used.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your data to anyone.
  • We do not show ads.
  • We do not share your data with any third parties beyond the Firebase infrastructure needed to run optional cloud sync.
  • We do not have access to your appointments, clients, or financial records.

Data collected by this website

This website does not collect personal data directly. If analytics are enabled in the future, only anonymous usage data (page views, referral source, device type) will be collected. You will be informed via the cookie banner before any tracking begins, and you can decline at any time.

Cookies

This website uses a single cookie to remember your analytics preference (accepted or declined). No tracking cookies are set without your explicit consent.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, edit, or delete any data you have entered in the app at any time.
  • Export a full copy of your data from the app settings (JSON format).
  • Delete all local data from the app settings.
  • Delete cloud-synced data from within the app or directly from your Firebase account.
  • Request complete data deletion by contacting us at the email address below.

Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the app. If you delete the app, local data is removed from your device. Cloud data (if sync was enabled) can be deleted from within the app before uninstalling, or by contacting us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The updated version will be published on this page with a revised date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests: c_mihail@icloud.com.
