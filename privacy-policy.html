Privacy Policy – Appointments & Reports

Last updated: April 2026. This policy covers the Appointments & Reports app (iOS/iPadOS & Android) and this website.

1. Data Controller & Developer

Appointments & Reports is developed and published by SOFTAPPS S.R.L., the data controller responsible for your personal data under GDPR.

SOFTAPPS S.R.L. (CUI: 54463640, Reg. Com.: J2026023970005, D-U-N-S: 303152744), with registered office at Aleea Sucidava Nr. 2, Iași 700442, Romania. For any privacy-related questions, contact us (contact@appointmentsapps.com / +40 749 798 510).

2. Data we collect

The app stores data you enter yourself:

  • Clients: name, phone number, email address, birthday, and notes.
  • Appointments: date, time, duration, service, price, and notes.
  • Financial records: income entries and expenses you add manually.
  • Settings: your preferences, working hours, reminder configurations, and service catalogue.

All of this data is created by you and belongs to you. When cloud sync is disabled, all data stays on your device and we do not collect, read, or process it. When cloud sync is enabled, data is stored in your personal Firebase account under your own user ID — see Section 4.

3. Permissions used

Contacts

  • The app can access your device contacts only when you explicitly tap "Import from Contacts" to create a new client.
  • Only the selected contact's name, phone number, and email are used.
  • Your full contacts list is never uploaded, stored, or shared.

Face ID / Biometric lock (iOS only)

  • Optional feature to protect access to the app using Face ID or Touch ID.
  • Biometric data never leaves your device and is never accessible to us.

Notifications

  • Used to send appointment reminders and birthday notifications you configure.
  • You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings.

4. Where your data is stored

  • Locally on your device — all data is stored on your phone or tablet. The app works fully offline.
  • Firebase (Google Cloud) — optional: if you sign in with your Google or Apple account, your data is synced to your personal Firebase account under your own user ID. This keeps your data in sync across your own devices. You can disable this at any time from the app settings.

5. Third-party services

The app uses the following services:

  • Google Sign-In / Firebase — for optional authentication and cloud sync. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Sign in with Apple (iOS only) — for optional authentication via Apple ID. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree (Formspree Inc., USA) — used to process sign-up requests submitted via the website contact form. Subject to Formspree's Privacy Policy (formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy).

No advertising SDKs or data brokers are used.

6. Online Booking — data submitted by appointment clients

If a service professional shares an online booking link generated by the app, their clients (end users) can submit a booking request via the website. The following data is collected at that point:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Selected service, date, and time
  • Optional note

This data is submitted directly to Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud) under the business owner's account and is accessible only to the business owner through the app. SOFTAPPS S.R.L. acts as a data processor on behalf of the business owner (data controller) for this data. The business owner is responsible for informing their clients about how their data is used. The legal basis for this processing is the performance of a contract (scheduling the requested appointment) under GDPR Art. 6(1)(b). This data is retained until the business owner deletes it from the app.

7. What we do NOT do

  • We do NOT sell your data to anyone.
  • We do NOT show ads.
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties beyond Firebase (cloud sync) and Formspree (website contact form).
  • We do NOT have access to your appointments, clients, or financial records.

8. Data collected by this website

This website does not collect personal data directly. If analytics are enabled, only anonymous usage data (page views, referral source, device type) will be collected. You will be informed via the cookie banner before any tracking begins, and you can decline at any time.

9. Cookies

This website uses a single cookie to remember your analytics preference (accepted or declined). No tracking cookies are set without your explicit consent.

10. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — view any data you entered in the app at any time.
  • Rectification — edit or correct your data directly in the app.
  • Erasure — delete all local data from the app settings, or request complete deletion by contacting us.
  • Data portability — export a full copy of your data from the app settings (JSON format), as provided under GDPR Art. 20.
  • Restriction of processing — request that we restrict the processing of your data under GDPR Art. 18.
  • Object to processing — object to processing based on legitimate interests under GDPR Art. 21.
  • Cloud data deletion — delete cloud-synced data from within the app or by contacting us.

11. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the app. If you delete the app, local data is removed from your device. Cloud data (if sync was enabled) can be deleted from within the app before uninstalling, or by contacting us.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The updated version will be published on this page with a revised date. For significant changes affecting your rights or the way we process your data, we will notify you via an in-app notice or by email (if we hold your email address).

13. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests: contact us (contact@appointmentsapps.com / +40 749 798 510).

14. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under GDPR Art. 6: (a) Performance of a contract — the data you enter is necessary to provide the app's core functionality (managing appointments, clients, and financial records); (b) Legitimate interests — anonymous analytics (if enabled) help us improve the app. You can withdraw consent at any time via the cookie banner.

15. International data transfers

If you enable cloud sync, your data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud), which may process data on servers outside the European Economic Area (EEA), including in the United States. Google complies with EU data transfer requirements through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs). For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

16. Supervisory authority & age restriction

  • If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (DPA). As SOFTAPPS S.R.L. is registered in Romania, the competent supervisory authority is ANSPDCP (Autoritatea Națională de Supraveghere a Prelucrării Datelor cu Caracter Personal) — www.dataprotection.ro.
  • This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
