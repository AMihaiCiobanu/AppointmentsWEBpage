Everything you need, built for how you actually work

All your tools in one place, easy to reach even when you're with a client. Sign in instantly with Google — no passwords to remember.

Smart appointment calendar

See who's coming, when, and for what service. The app suggests your first available slot automatically, prevents double-bookings, and lets you set recurring days off or vacations.

Full client history at a glance

All contact info and visit history in one place. Add notes on preferences or allergies, flag unreliable clients, and get automatic birthday reminders so you never miss a personal touch.

Revenue tracked automatically

Every appointment you book automatically creates an income entry. Add expenses in one tap — rent, supplies, anything. Your daily profit is calculated instantly, no spreadsheets needed.

4-mode calendar view

Switch the same calendar between four views: availability, income, expenses, and profit — all in one screen. See your business performance day by day at a glance.

Ready-made confirmation messages

Send appointment confirmations via SMS or WhatsApp in one tap. Templates are ready to go — date, time, and your social links inserted automatically.

Lock screen widget iOS

Your next appointment shows directly on your iPhone lock screen via Live Activities — client name, service, and time, without even unlocking your phone.

We understand how sensitive your client list and financial records are. Everything is designed to give you complete peace of mind.
